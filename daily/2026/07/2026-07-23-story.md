## 全英文版

In a peculiar kingdom, a persistent and eccentric king was determined to maintain his delicate porcelain skin. He firmly believed that death was an inevitable cliché, something entirely irrelevant to a ruler of his stature. To combat what he called “time bacteria,” he demanded that the royal sorcerer must persist in bathing the palace rooftops daily with unicorn tears. The king even chose to commit all his holidays to personally overseeing this peculiar ritual. However, one sunny afternoon, a truly spontaneous rain of multi-colored bubbles unexpectedly descended, blanketing the entire capital city. Witnessing this strange phenomenon, the sorcerer vowed to dedicate the remainder of his life to studying its magical properties. Consequently, the king discovered that his own hair had transformed into living spaghetti, constantly growing. The courtiers hastily devised an absurd strategy: they fashioned a colossal spaghetti hat for the king using gigantic baking tools, hoping to conceal this strange secret. Within the castle, public awareness of this bizarre incident grew in an unwritten proportion to the length of the king’s noodle-hair. Meanwhile, the king’s pet pig inexplicably began to sing, its voice like a chorus of shattered bells.

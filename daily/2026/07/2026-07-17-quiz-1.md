# 2026-07-17｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. The committee met to ________ the ongoing dispute.

A. A. complicate
B. B. ignore
C. C. discuss
D. D. resolve

## 2. Which sentence correctly uses the word "involve"?

A. A. The recipe involves three eggs.
B. B. He involves in the party.
C. C. They involves a new idea.
D. D. We were involving to help.

## 3. "当前的趋势" 的正确英语表达是：

A. A. present trend
B. B. future trend
C. C. past trend
D. D. current trend

## 4. A "concept" is primarily a/an:

A. A. tangible object
B. B. abstract idea
C. C. physical law
D. D. historical event

## 5. Politeness is a common ________ of Japanese culture.

A. A. aspect
B. B. characteristic
C. C. attribute
D. D. E. element

## 6. Her deep ________ into human nature made her a great writer.

A. A. view
B. B. glance
C. C. insight
D. D. observation

## 7. What does it mean to "participate in a competition"?

A. A. 赢得比赛
B. B. 观看比赛
C. C. 参加比赛
D. D. 组织比赛

## 8. "放弃希望" 最合适的英语表达是：

A. A. give up hope
B. B. release hope
C. C. abandon hope
D. D. leave hope

## 9. It takes time for people to ________ to a new environment.

A. A. adjust
B. B. adapt
C. C. apply
D. D. accustom

## 10. It's hard to ________ between the identical twins.

A. A. distinguish
B. B. identify
C. C. separate
D. D. compare

## 答案与解析

1. **D**｜resolve 作为动词意为“解决（问题或争端）”。
2. **A**｜involve常用于指某事包含或需要某物作为组成部分，句型为 A involves B。
3. **D**｜current 作为形容词意为“当前的，现在的”。
4. **B**｜concept 指的是抽象的观念或概念。
5. **B**｜characteristic 指的是某个事物独有的、易于辨认的特征。
6. **C**｜insight 指的是对事物本质的深刻理解或洞察力。
7. **C**｜participate in something 意为“参与或参加某事”。
8. **C**｜abandon hope 是“放弃希望”的常见搭配。
9. **B**｜adapt to 意为“适应新的环境或条件”。
10. **A**｜distinguish between 意为“区分、辨别”两者之间的不同。

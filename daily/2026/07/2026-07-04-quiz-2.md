# 2026-07-04｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. She has great _______ as an artist, but she needs more practice.

A. A. ability
B. B. talent
C. C. potential
D. D. skill

## 2. What is the most likely _______ of climate change?

A. A. impact
B. B. cause
C. C. origin
D. D. solution

## 3. Many ancient cultures _______ supernatural powers to the sun and moon.

A. A. contributed
B. B. attributed
C. C. ascribed
D. D. distributed

## 4. The police found no _______ to link him to the crime.

A. A. motive
B. B. evidence
C. C. alibi
D. D. suspect

## 5. Which of the following sentences uses "optional" correctly?

A. A. The optional subject is mandatory for all students.
B. B. You have the optional to choose your own dessert.
C. C. Wearing a uniform is optional at this school.
D. D. They made an optional decision to stay home.

## 6. The chameleon can _______ its color to blend in with its surroundings.

A. A. adjust
B. B. convert
C. C. adapt
D. D. modify

## 7. I don't want to go out tonight; _______, I have a lot of work to do.

A. A. however
B. B. therefore
C. C. instead
D. D. besides

## 8. The company suffered a _______ loss due to the economic downturn.

A. A. minor
B. B. negligible
C. C. significant
D. D. trivial

## 9. How would you translate "为紧急情况分拨资金" into English?

A. A. Assign funds for emergency
B. B. Allocate funds for emergencies
C. C. Distribute funds for emergency
D. D. Dispatch funds for emergencies

## 10. To _______ a healthy lifestyle, regular exercise and a balanced diet are essential.

A. A. obtain
B. B. acquire
C. C. maintain
D. D. improve

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜“potential”指的是某人未来可能发展或实现的能力或可能性，符合句中艺术家有很大潜力的语境。
2. **A**｜“consequence”指结果、后果，在此处与“impact”（影响）作为近义词最贴切。
3. **B**｜“attribute (to)”意为把...归因于，在此处表示将超自然力量归因于日月。
4. **B**｜句子表示警方没有发现将他与罪行联系起来的证据，因此选择“evidence”。
5. **C**｜“optional”作形容词，意为可选的，非强制的。“Wearing a uniform is optional”表述正确，符合其形容词的用法。
6. **C**｜“adapt”在这里指适应环境，变色以融入周围环境，强调自身做出改变以适应。
7. **D**｜“moreover”意为此外、而且，表示附加信息，与“besides”含义相近。
8. **C**｜“considerable”意为相当大的，与“significant”同义，符合句子中因经济衰退而遭受巨大损失的语境。
9. **B**｜“Allocate funds for emergencies”是“为紧急情况分拨资金”的正确翻译，其中“allocate”意为分配，分拨。
10. **C**｜“maintain”意为维持、保持，符合语境中保持健康生活方式的含义。

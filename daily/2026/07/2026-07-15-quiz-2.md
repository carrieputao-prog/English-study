# 2026-07-15｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. "洞察力" 对应的英语单词是：

A. A. perception
B. B. overview
C. C. insight
D. D. observation

## 2. The director decided to ________ the novel for the big screen.

A. A. adopt
B. B. adjust
C. C. adapt
D. D. adhere

## 3. The job will ________ frequent travel.

A. A. exclude
B. B. involve
C. C. release
D. D. neglect

## 4. 选择最接近 "distinguish" 词义的选项。

A. A. differentiate
B. B. confuse
C. C. combine
D. D. overlook

## 5. If you "resolve to do something," what do you do?

A. A. give up on doing it
B. B. firmly decide to do it
C. C. ask someone else to do it
D. D. postpone doing it

## 6. Which sentence uses "current" as a noun?

A. A. What is your current address?
B. B. The strong current pulled the boat downstream.
C. C. We need to check the current situation.
D. D. He is keeping up with current events.

## 7. To "abandon ship" means to:

A. A. repair the ship
B. B. leave the ship permanently
C. C. clean the ship
D. D. take control of the ship

## 8. “万有引力定律是一个重要的科学_______。” 空格处应填入哪个词？

A. A. insight
B. B. concept
C. C. characteristic
D. D. current

## 9. Which word is an antonym of "participate"?

A. A. join
B. B. withdraw
C. C. engage
D. D. contribute

## 10. 哪个词是 "characteristic" 的近义词？

A. A. feature
B. B. flaw
C. C. similarity
D. D. standard

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜Insight 的意思是洞察力，与“洞察力”直接对应。
2. **C**｜Adapt 意为改编，在此语境中指将小说改编成电影。
3. **B**｜Involve 在此句中表示“包含”或“涉及”，与语境相符。
4. **A**｜Distinguish 的意思是区别、辨别，与 differentiate 同义。
5. **B**｜Resolve 作为动词有“决定”的意思，resolve to do something 表示坚定地决定做某事。
6. **B**｜在选项 B 中，current 作为名词表示水流，其他选项中 current 均作形容词。
7. **B**｜Abandon 的意思是放弃、遗弃，"abandon ship" 指永久地离开船只。
8. **B**｜这里指“科学概念”，因此 concept 是最合适的词。
9. **B**｜Participate 意为参与，withdraw 意为退出，是其反义词。
10. **A**｜Characteristic 指特征、特色，feature 是其近义词。

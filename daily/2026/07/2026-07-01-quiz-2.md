# 2026-07-01｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. The project required a _______ amount of time and effort to complete.

A. A) tiny
B. B) minimal
C. C) considerable
D. D) slight

## 2. Which of the following is the best Chinese translation for "scientific evidence"?

A. A) 科学证明
B. B) 科学假设
C. C) 科学理论
D. D) 科学研究

## 3. The government decided to _______ a significant portion of the budget to education and healthcare.

A. A) donate
B. B) collect
C. C) allocate
D. D) propose

## 4. Which of the following best translates the meaning of "maintain peace" into Chinese?

A. A) 保持和平
B. B) 提出和平
C. C) 损坏和平
D. D) 结束和平

## 5. She tends to _______ her success to hard work and perseverance rather than luck.

A. A) contribute
B. B) dedicate
C. C) attribute
D. D) ascribe

## 6. What is the primary meaning of "adapt" in the sentence: "The director decided to adapt the novel into a film."?

A. A) 调整
B. B) 适应
C. C) 改编
D. D) 匹配

## 7. Which of the following best translates the phrase "take responsibility for the consequences"?

A. A) 对结果负责
B. B) 了解结果
C. C) 忽视结果
D. D) 创造结果

## 8. Which sentence correctly uses the word "potential" as an adjective?

A. A) The potential of the new technology is enormous.
B. B) There is a potential risk involved in the project.
C. C) Her potential for growth is very high.
D. D) He realized his full potential after years of training.

## 9. Attending the workshop is _______, but highly recommended for all new employees.

A. A) compulsory
B. B) optional
C. C) mandatory
D. D) essential

## 10. Which of the following words has a similar function to "moreover" in a sentence?

A. A) however
B. B) therefore
C. C) in addition
D. D) nevertheless

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜句意是项目需要“相当多的”时间精力来完成，“considerable”符合语境。
2. **A**｜"scientific evidence" 意为 "科学证据" 或 "科学证明"。
3. **C**｜根据句意，政府决定将预算的很大一部分“分配”给教育和医疗，因此“allocate”是正确选项。
4. **A**｜"maintain peace" 意为“保持和平”或“维持和平”。
5. **C**｜句意是她倾向于将成功归因于努力工作和毅力，“attribute to”意为“把…归因于”。
6. **C**｜在此语境中，“adapt”指将小说“改编”成电影。
7. **A**｜"take responsibility for the consequences" 意为“对结果负责”。
8. **B**｜在B选项中，“potential”修饰“risk”作为形容词，表示“潜在的风险”。
9. **B**｜句意是参加研讨会是“可选择的”但强烈推荐，因此“optional”符合语境。
10. **C**｜“moreover”和“in addition”都用于在句子中添加额外信息，表示“此外，而且”。

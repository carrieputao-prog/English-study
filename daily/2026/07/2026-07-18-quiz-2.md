# 2026-07-18｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. Many students choose to ________ in extracurricular activities to enrich their school life.

A. A. decline
B. B. involve
C. C. participate
D. D. avoid

## 2. She made a firm ________ to finish her novel by the end of the year.

A. A. resolve
B. B. doubt
C. C. delay
D. D. issue

## 3. The film director decided to ________ the best-selling novel into a screenplay for a wider audience.

A. A. invent
B. B. adapt
C. C. ignore
D. D. criticize

## 4. The powerful river ________ carried the small boat downstream rapidly.

A. A. stream
B. B. flow
C. C. current
D. D. wave

## 5. Which of the following best describes the action of "distinguish"?

A. A. To mix things together
B. B. To identify differences between things
C. C. To ignore minor details
D. D. To group similar items

## 6. A key ________ of a good leader is the ability to inspire and motivate team members.

A. A. consequence
B. B. characteristic
C. C. drawback
D. D. limitation

## 7. The author's latest book offers profound ________ into the complexities of human relationships.

A. A. doubt
B. B. insight
C. C. surface
D. D. prejudice

## 8. 创新通常始于一个新颖的________。 (Fill in the blank with the best English word)

A. A. method
B. B. result
C. C. concept
D. D. process

## 9. Due to the sudden heavy rain, they had to ________ their plans for the outdoor picnic.

A. A. continue
B. B. reconsider
C. C. abandon
D. D. improve

## 10. The director explained that the new play would ________ a large cast and crew. (Translate the underlined word)

A. A. 包括
B. B. 避免
C. C. 简化
D. D. 分配

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜"participate in" 意为“参与，参加”，符合句意。
2. **A**｜"resolve" 作为名词，意为“决心，决意”，符合句中做出坚定的决心的语境。
3. **B**｜"adapt" 意为“改编”，常用于将小说、剧本等改编成电影。
4. **C**｜"current" 作为名词意为“水流”，特指有方向和力量的水流，符合句意。
5. **B**｜"distinguish" 意为“辨别，识别出不同”。
6. **B**｜"characteristic" 意为“特征，特色”，是描述领导者能力的关键词。
7. **B**｜"insight" 意为“洞察力，见识”，符合对人际关系复杂性的深刻理解。
8. **C**｜"concept" 意为“概念，观念”，与“创新”和“新颖的”搭配合理。
9. **C**｜"abandon" 意为“放弃”，符合因下雨而不得不放弃野餐计划的语境。
10. **A**｜"involve" 在此句中意为“包含，涉及”，故选“包括”。

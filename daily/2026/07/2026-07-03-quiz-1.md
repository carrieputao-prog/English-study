# 2026-07-03｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. If something is "optional", it means it is _______.

A. A. mandatory
B. B. required
C. C. compulsory
D. D. not compulsory

## 2. "合理分配资源"的最佳英文表达是？

A. A. reasonably divide resources
B. B. fairly share resources
C. C. rationally allocate resources
D. D. properly distribute resources

## 3. The young athlete shows great _______ to become a champion.

A. A. power
B. B. potential
C. C. strength
D. D. ability

## 4. There is no concrete _______ to support his claims.

A. A. evidence
B. B. guess
C. C. idea
D. D. rumor

## 5. It's important to regularly _______ your car for safety.

A. A. repair
B. B. improve
C. C. maintain
D. D. build

## 6. Failure to follow the rules will have serious _______. 

A. A. solutions
B. B. consequences
C. C. beginnings
D. D. advantages

## 7. The hotel offers excellent service; _______, it's located in the city center.

A. A. however
B. B. therefore
C. C. moreover
D. D. despite

## 8. Which of the following is NOT an _______ of a good leader?

A. A. honesty
B. B. responsibility
C. C. laziness
D. D. vision

## 9. It took her a while to _______ to the new working environment.

A. A. adjust
B. B. accustom
C. C. adapt
D. D. conform

## 10. Which word is closest in meaning to "considerable"?

A. A. slight
B. B. trivial
C. C. significant
D. D. minimal

## 答案与解析

1. **D**｜"optional" 意为可选择的，非强制的，因此它意味着“非强制的”。
2. **C**｜"allocate" 意为分配，常用于资源、任务等的分配，"rationally" 意为合理地。
3. **B**｜句子表达“有很大的潜力成为冠军”，"potential"在此用作名词表示潜力。
4. **A**｜句子缺少一个表示"证据"的名词来支持"他的主张"。
5. **C**｜"maintain" 意为维修或保养，符合句意中对汽车进行定期保养以保证安全。
6. **B**｜"consequences" 意为后果，通常指不好的结果，符合句意中不遵守规则会带来严重的后果。
7. **C**｜"moreover" 意为此外、而且，用于添加额外信息或论据，符合句意中在提到服务好之后，补充说明地理位置优越。
8. **C**｜"attribute" 在此用作名词，意为属性或特征，而“懒惰”显然不是好领导的属性。
9. **C**｜"adapt to" 意为适应，符合句意中适应新的工作环境。
10. **C**｜"considerable" 意为相当大的，与"significant"（重要的，显著的）意思最接近。

# 2026-07-06｜香蕉的艺术人生

## 本轮 10 个单词

therefore（因此） · consistent（一致的，连贯的） · evaluate（评估，评价） · dedicate（致力于，奉献） · obtain（获得，得到） · exclusive（独有的，排外的） · release（释放；发布） · convey（表达，传达（思想/感情）） · obstacle（障碍，妨碍） · commit（犯罪；承诺，把...交托）

## 中英穿插故事

一位古怪的植物学家决定将他全部精力 dedicate（奉献） 给一项异想天开的任务：培育出世界上第一根会自动剥皮的、会歌唱的香蕉。为了 obtain（获得） 这种独一无二的植物，他夜以继日地在温室里工作。这项技术将是他的 exclusive（独有） 秘密，不与任何人分享。主要的 obstacle（障碍） 是如何让植物表达情感并自行完成复杂动作。他必须不断 evaluate（评估） 失败的实验数据，调整基因序列。他对这项事业的痴迷是 consistent（一致的），从不间断。therefore（因此），在一个月黑风高的夜晚，一根闪闪发光的香蕉突然开始哼唱小夜曲，并优雅地为自己剥去了一半皮。植物学家激动地试图向全世界 convey（传达） 它的成功和这根香蕉的“喜悦”。他决定在电视直播中 release（发布） 这根香蕉，向大众展示奇迹。然而，这根香蕉在聚光灯下突然宣布它将 commit（投身） 艺术事业，成为一名著名的歌剧演员，而不是被吃掉。

## 全英文版

An eccentric botanist decided to dedicate his entire life to an extraordinary task: cultivating the world's first self-peeling, singing banana. He worked tirelessly in his greenhouse to obtain this unique plant. This revolutionary technique would be his exclusive secret, shared with no one. The primary obstacle was figuring out how a plant could express emotion and perform complex actions autonomously. He constantly had to evaluate failed experimental data, meticulously adjusting gene sequences. His obsession with this endeavor remained remarkably consistent, never wavering. Therefore, on a dark and stormy night, a glistening banana suddenly began to hum a lullaby and gracefully peeled itself halfway. The botanist excitedly tried to convey its success and the banana's "joy" to the world. He decided to release this wondrous banana on live television, presenting a miracle to the public. However, under the spotlight, the banana unexpectedly announced its decision to commit to an artistic career, becoming a renowned opera singer, rather than merely being eaten.

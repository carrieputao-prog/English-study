# 2026-07-08｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. The main purpose of language is to ______ ideas effectively.

A. evaluate
B. obtain
C. convey
D. commit

## 2. "Obtain" in "to obtain a permit" is closest in meaning to:

A. acquire
B. prevent
C. hide
D. give up

## 3. Which option correctly translates "release pressure"?

A. 释放压力
B. 增加压力
C. 承受压力
D. 保持压力

## 4. He was arrested for attempting to ______ a robbery.

A. evaluate
B. dedicate
C. release
D. commit

## 5. Which sentence correctly uses the word "exclusive"?

A. This store has the exclusive right to sell the product in this region.
B. The general public can enter this exclusive area.
C. He felt inclusive from the group.
D. They offered a non-exclusive deal to everyone.

## 6. The artist decided to ______ her latest masterpiece to her mentor.

A. commit
B. release
C. convey
D. dedicate

## 7. Before making a big purchase, it's wise to carefully ______ all your options.

A. obtain
B. release
C. evaluate
D. dedicate

## 8. 选择最能表达“因此”含义的词语是？

A. However
B. Therefore
C. Moreover
D. Although

## 9. Her actions are not ______ with her words. Which word best fits the blank?

A. consistent
B. evaluating
C. obtaining
D. releasing

## 10. "Obstacle" in "overcome obstacles" is closest in meaning to:

A. advantage
B. hindrance
C. solution
D. opportunity

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜"convey ideas" 意为“传达思想”，符合句意。
2. **A**｜"obtain" 意为“获得，得到”，与 "acquire" 意思相近。
3. **A**｜"release pressure" 意为“释放压力”，符合词义。
4. **D**｜"commit a robbery" 意为“实施抢劫”，符合词义。
5. **A**｜"exclusive right" 意为“独家权利”，表示排他性，符合词义。
6. **D**｜"dedicate something to someone" 意为“把...献给某人”，符合句意。
7. **C**｜"evaluate options" 意为“评估所有选项”，符合句意。
8. **B**｜"therefore" 意为“因此，所以”，表示结果。
9. **A**｜"consistent with" 意为“与...一致”，符合句意。
10. **B**｜"obstacle" 意为“障碍，妨碍”，与 "hindrance" 意思相近。

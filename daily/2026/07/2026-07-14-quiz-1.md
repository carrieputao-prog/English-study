# 2026-07-14｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. 哪个词最能表达“放弃”的意思？

A. A) release
B. B) preserve
C. C) abandon
D. D) maintain

## 2. What is the meaning of "characteristic"?

A. A) 关键
B. B) 特征
C. C) 组成
D. D) 优点

## 3. 哪个词可以表示“适应新环境”中的“适应”？

A. A) adjust
B. B) conform
C. C) adapt
D. D) regulate

## 4. Which of the following best describes the word "concept"?

A. A) 结论
B. B) 概念
C. C) 证明
D. D) 方法

## 5. Which sentence uses "involve" correctly?

A. A) His job involves a lot of travelling.
B. B) He involved in the project.
C. C) She involves in too much work.
D. D) The movie involved them greatly.

## 6. Her speech offered a deep ________ into the challenges of modern parenting.

A. A) view
B. B) opinion
C. C) insight
D. D) thought

## 7. The committee met to ________ the ongoing dispute.

A. A) solve
B. B) settle
C. C) determine
D. D) resolve

## 8. In which sentence is "current" used as an adjective?

A. A) The strong current swept the boat downstream.
B. B) We need to address the current issues.
C. C) She felt a powerful electric current.
D. D) Divert the current of public opinion.

## 9. It is often difficult to ________ right from wrong.

A. A) choose
B. B) prefer
C. C) distinguish
D. D) select

## 10. Everyone is encouraged to ________ in the discussion.

A. A) join
B. B) take
C. C) involve
D. D) participate

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜'abandon' 的意思是“放弃，遗弃”。
2. **B**｜'characteristic' 的意思是“特征，特色”。
3. **C**｜'adapt to' 意为“适应（新环境、新情况）”。
4. **B**｜'concept' 的意思是“概念，观念”。
5. **A**｜'involve' 作为及物动词，常用于 'involve sth' 或 'involve doing sth'，意为“包含，牵涉”。
6. **C**｜'insight' 意为“洞察力，深刻的见解”，常与 'into' 搭配。
7. **D**｜'resolve' 意为“解决（问题、争端）”。
8. **B**｜在选项B中，'current' 修饰名词 'issues'，作形容词，意为“当前的”。其他选项中 'current' 作名词。
9. **C**｜'distinguish A from B' 意为“区分A和B”。
10. **D**｜'participate in' 意为“参与某事”。

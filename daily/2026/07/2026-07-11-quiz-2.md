# 2026-07-11｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. The lawyer presented strong _______ to the jury, hoping to win the case.

A. A) opinions
B. B) doubts
C. C) evidence
D. D) rumors

## 2. Which word is an antonym for "abstract"?

A. A) conceptual
B. B) concrete
C. C) theoretical
D. D) vague

## 3. The university decided to _______ more funds to the science department this year.

A. A) detach
B. B) donate
C. C) allocate
D. D) subtract

## 4. The new building design ensures that all areas are easily _______ to wheelchair users.

A. A) invisible
B. B) inaccessible
C. C) accessible
D. D) remote

## 5. What does it mean if something is "beneficial"?

A. A) It causes harm.
B. B) It has a negative impact.
C. C) It provides advantages or is helpful.
D. D) It is irrelevant.

## 6. It is important to _______ the root cause of the problem to find an effective solution.

A. A) ignore
B. B) disguise
C. C) identify
D. D) overlook

## 7. The new policy will reduce costs; _______, it will improve efficiency.

A. A) nevertheless
B. B) although
C. C) furthermore
D. D) despite

## 8. I enjoy hiking; _______, I prefer to do it in cooler weather.

A. A) in addition
B. B) therefore
C. C) however
D. D) similarly

## 9. Which of the following is NOT an "essential" element for plant growth?

A. A) Sunlight
B. B) Water
C. C) Soil
D. D) Decoration

## 10. The small café cannot _______ large groups of people during peak hours.

A. A) refuse
B. B) reject
C. C) accommodate
D. D) expel

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜"evidence" 意为证据，在此句中指律师向陪审团提供有力证据。
2. **B**｜"abstract" 意为抽象的，其反义词是 "concrete"（具体的）。
3. **C**｜"allocate" 意为分配，在此句中指大学决定将更多资金分配给科学系。
4. **C**｜"accessible" 意为易接近的，可进入的，在此句中指建筑的所有区域都方便轮椅使用者进入。
5. **C**｜"beneficial" 意为有益的，有利的，因此它提供优势或有帮助。
6. **C**｜"identify" 意为确认、查明，在此句中指查明问题的根本原因。
7. **C**｜"furthermore" 意为此外、而且，用于连接并补充信息。
8. **C**｜"however" 意为然而，在此句中表示转折，指出偏好。
9. **D**｜"essential" 意为必要的，装饰品（decoration）对于植物生长来说不是必要的元素。
10. **C**｜"accommodate" 意为容纳，在此句中指咖啡馆无法容纳大量人群。

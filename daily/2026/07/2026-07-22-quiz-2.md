# 2026-07-22｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. When you move to a new country, you need to _____ to different customs and ways of life.

A. A. resist
B. B. adjust
C. C. ignore
D. D. insist

## 2. Which word best describes a person who always tries their best and never gives up?

A. A. inconsistent
B. B. sporadic
C. C. consistent
D. D. erratic

## 3. The artist used vibrant colors to _____ a sense of joy and optimism in her painting.

A. A. conceal
B. B. obscure
C. C. convey
D. D. distort

## 4. Which of the following best translates "除了这些，他的演讲还非常鼓舞人心"?

A. A. Beside these, his speech was very inspiring.
B. B. Moreover, his speech was very inspiring.
C. C. Despite these, his speech was very inspiring.
D. D. However, his speech was very inspiring.

## 5. If the main power fails, we have an _____ power source.

A. A. exclusive
B. B. primary
C. C. alternative
D. D. permanent

## 6. Making a _____ decision quickly is essential for the success of the project.

A. A. optional
B. B. critical
C. C. minor
D. D. redundant

## 7. Patience is a key _____ for anyone working in customer service.

A. A. setback
B. B. attribute
C. C. hindrance
D. D. disadvantage

## 8. Which of the following best translates "He showed considerable improvement in his performance."?

A. A. 他在表现上没有明显进步。
B. B. 他的表现有了显著的进步。
C. C. 他的表现差强人意。
D. D. 他对自己的表现感到满意。

## 9. The severe storm is expected to _____ the flight schedules.

A. A. avoid
B. B. impact
C. C. support
D. D. neglect

## 10. Which of the following best translates "release the tension"?

A. A. 释放紧张
B. B. 增加压力
C. C. 保持稳定
D. D. 限制自由

## 答案与解析

1. **B**｜Adjust在此句中意为“适应”。
2. **C**｜Consistent在此句中意为“始终如一的，坚持不懈的”。
3. **C**｜Convey在此句中作动词，意为“表达，传达”。
4. **B**｜Moreover意为“此外，而且”，用于引出额外的信息或论点，符合句意。
5. **C**｜Alternative在此句中作形容词，意为“备用的，可供选择的”。
6. **B**｜Critical在此句中意为“关键的，至关重要的”。
7. **B**｜Attribute在此句中作名词，意为“属性，特征”。
8. **B**｜Considerable意为“相当大的，显著的”。
9. **B**｜Impact在此句中作动词，意为“影响”。
10. **A**｜Release the tension意为“释放压力，放松”。

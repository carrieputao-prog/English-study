# 2026-07-21｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. Which of the following best translates "新电影的发布日期"?

A. A. the new movie's launch date
B. B. the new movie's delivery date
C. C. the new movie's release date
D. D. the new movie's issue date

## 2. The new policy is designed to reduce pollution; _____, it aims to create more job opportunities.

A. A. however
B. B. therefore
C. C. moreover
D. D. nevertheless

## 3. Her actions are not _____ with her stated beliefs; they often contradict each other.

A. A. relevant
B. B. consistent
C. C. coincident
D. D. comparable

## 4. The successful outcome of the project was largely _____ to the team's hard work.

A. A. contributed
B. B. attributed
C. C. resulted
D. D. committed

## 5. The company has invested a _____ amount of money in research and development.

A. A. considerable
B. B. considerate
C. C. consulting
D. D. consuming

## 6. Which of the following best translates "你需要调整你的计划以适应新的情况"?

A. A. You need to arrange your plan to suit the new situation.
B. B. You need to adapt your plan to suit the new situation.
C. C. You need to adjust your plan to suit the new situation.
D. D. You need to reform your plan to suit the new situation.

## 7. She struggled to find the right words to _____ her true feelings to him.

A. A. convey
B. B. confer
C. C. confirm
D. D. convince

## 8. The doctor stated that the patient's condition was _____, requiring immediate surgery.

A. A. casual
B. B. critical
C. C. calm
D. D. comfortable

## 9. We need to find an _____ solution to this problem, as the current one is not working.

A. A. alternative
B. B. alterable
C. C. altering
D. D. altered

## 10. Climate change will have a significant _____ on the global economy and environment.

A. A. impact
B. B. effect
C. C. influence
D. D. impression

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜Release 作动词或名词时，常用于指电影、音乐、产品等的“发布”。
2. **C**｜Moreover 意为“此外，而且”，用于添加额外的信息或论据。
3. **B**｜Consistent with 意为“与……一致的”，符合句意。
4. **B**｜Attribute to 意为“把……归因于”，符合句子表达因果关系的需求。
5. **A**｜Considerable 意为“相当大的，相当多的”，符合句中形容金额的语境。
6. **C**｜Adjust 意为“调整”，常用于调整计划、设置等。
7. **A**｜Convey 意为“表达，传达（思想、感情、信息）”，符合句意。
8. **B**｜Critical 在这里表示“危急的，严重的”，形容病人的状况。
9. **A**｜Alternative 作形容词意为“可供替代的”，修饰 solution。
10. **A**｜Impact 作名词意为“影响，作用”，常用于描述重大影响。

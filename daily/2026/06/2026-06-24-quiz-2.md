# 2026-06-24｜10 词自测（第 2 套）

## 1. What does 'approach' mean in the sentence: 'As winter approaches, the days get shorter'?

A. A. 远去
B. B. 变暖
C. C. 临近
D. D. 离开

## 2. Which sentence correctly uses 'furthermore' to add additional information?

A. A. He didn't like the food, furthermore, he left early.
B. B. The book was expensive; furthermore, it was poorly written.
C. C. She furthermore excels in sports.
D. D. Furthermore, what should we do next?

## 3. What is the best Chinese translation for 'participate'?

A. A. 放弃
B. B. 组织
C. C. 参与
D. D. 观察

## 4. The phrase '提供住宿' can be translated as which of the following?

A. A. refuse shelter
B. B. deny stay
C. C. accommodate
D. D. manage hotel

## 5. What does 'distinguish' mean in the sentence: 'It's important to distinguish between fact and opinion'?

A. A. 混合
B. B. 识别
C. C. 忽略
D. D. 接受

## 6. Which English word is the best translation for '特征' in the context of a distinguishing quality?

A. A. attribute
B. B. quality
C. C. characteristic
D. D. element

## 7. Which word is a synonym for 'bear' meaning 'to carry or support'?

A. A. drop
B. B. hold
C. C. forget
D. D. remove

## 8. Which English word means '有益的，有利的'?

A. A. harmful
B. B. beneficial
C. C. useless
D. D. critical

## 9. In the sentence 'You need to adjust to the new environment', what does 'adjust' mean?

A. A. 离开
B. B. 适应
C. C. 改变
D. D. 享受

## 10. What is the meaning of 'abandon' in the sentence: 'They had to abandon their car on the side of the road'?

A. A. 修理
B. B. 遗弃
C. C. 驾驶
D. D. 销售

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜在此句中，approach 指冬天“临近”的意思。
2. **B**｜Furthermore通常用于连接两个独立的句子或子句，表示“此外，而且”，补充说明信息。
3. **C**｜participate的中文意思是“参与，参加”。
4. **C**｜accommodate的其中一个意思是“提供住宿”。
5. **B**｜在此句中，distinguish between 意为“辨别、区分”事实和观点。
6. **C**｜characteristic意为“特征，特色”，是“特征”最直接的翻译。
7. **B**｜在“忍受；承担；支撑”的语境下，hold可以作为bear的近义词，意为支撑、承受。
8. **B**｜beneficial的中文意思正是“有益的，有利的”。
9. **B**｜在此语境中，adjust to the new environment 意为适应新环境。
10. **B**｜句子中abandon指因为故障等原因将车留在路边，意为遗弃。

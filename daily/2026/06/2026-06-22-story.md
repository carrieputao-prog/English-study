## 中英穿插故事

曾几何时，一只灰熊培养了一种奇特的艺术characteristic（特征，特色）。它厌倦了平淡无奇的森林生活，决定abandon（放弃）野外。它找到了一家前卫艺术画廊，渴望participate（参与，参加）其充满活力的文化。它独特的艺术欣赏approach（方法，途径；靠近）包括仔细嗅探画布，偶尔还会舔舐特别鲜艳的油画。古怪的画廊老板看到了媒体潜力，试图accommodate（容纳；提供住宿）这只不寻常的顾客，认为它的出现对宣传将是beneficial（有益的，有利的）。他们不得不adjust（调整；适应）整个展览布局，加固基座并添加蜂蜜陷阱。Furthermore（此外，而且），他们试图distinguish（区别，辨别）这只熊更喜欢哪种抽象表现主义作品，根据其爪印的深浅来判断。这只现在名为“Artie”的熊，不得不bear（忍受；承担；结（果实））无休止的闪光灯摄影和人类的赞叹气息，这是它获得新艺术自由的小小代价。

## 全英文版

A highly unusual grizzly bear, exhibiting a unique artistic characteristic, grew bored with its mundane forest existence. It chose to abandon the wild in favor of culture. Seeking a more intellectually stimulating life, it ventured into a bustling city and discovered a contemporary art gallery. It was determined to participate in the human art scene. The bear's unconventional approach to art appreciation involved carefully sniffing canvases and occasionally licking particularly vibrant oil paintings. The gallery's owner, always seeking novel publicity, decided to accommodate the furry connoisseur. She believed its presence would be immensely beneficial, drawing crowds and media attention. To ensure "Artie" (as the bear was named) had a comfortable experience, staff had to adjust the gallery's entire environment, installing reinforced barriers and placing oversized chew toys discreetly near delicate installations. Furthermore, experts were hired to distinguish between Artie's genuine artistic reactions and mere hunger cues. Artie seemed to bear the constant human gaze with stoic grace, a true artist enduring the eccentricities of fame, occasionally letting out a contented growl that sounded suspiciously like a critique.

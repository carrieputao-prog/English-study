# 2026-06-26｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. Which of the following best translates the word "abstract"?

A. A. 具体的
B. B. 抽象的
C. C. 概括的
D. D. 详细的

## 2. A healthy diet and regular exercise are _______ for a long life.

A. A. optional
B. B. vital
C. C. trivial
D. D. secondary

## 3. If you don't like coffee, there are plenty of ______ like tea or juice.

A. A. choices
B. B. alternatives
C. C. selections
D. D. options

## 4. The ______ of women in the workforce has increased significantly.

A. A. total
B. B. amount
C. C. proportion
D. D. number

## 5. The weather was terrible; _______, we still decided to go for a hike.

A. A. therefore
B. B. moreover
C. C. however
D. D. consequently

## 6. Which sentence uses "impact" correctly as a noun meaning collision or effect?

A. A. The meteor had a significant impact on the Earth's surface.
B. B. His new book impact greatly on its readers.
C. C. She likes to impact the ball with a strong swing.
D. D. We need to impact our thoughts to the manager.

## 7. Which of the following words is a synonym for "inevitable"?

A. A. avoidable
B. B. unlikely
C. C. certain
D. D. flexible

## 8. The company had to ______ responsibility for the data breach.

A. A. proclaim
B. B. explain
C. C. claim
D. D. exclaim

## 9. You should study hard; _______, you might fail the exam.

A. A. although
B. B. therefore
C. C. otherwise
D. D. nonetheless

## 10. Which option best translates "公司需要一个新的市场策略"?

A. A. The company needs a new market tactics.
B. B. The company needs a new market method.
C. C. The company needs a new market strategy.
D. D. The company needs a new market plan.

## 答案与解析

1. **B**｜"abstract" 的意思是抽象的，与 "具体的" 相对。
2. **B**｜"essential" 意为必要的、不可或缺的，与 "vital" 意思相近。
3. **B**｜"alternatives" 指可供选择的替代品。
4. **C**｜"proportion" 意为部分、比例，用于表示在总量中所占的份额。
5. **C**｜"however" 表示转折关系，与前一句形成对比。
6. **A**｜选项A中 "impact" 作为名词表示撞击或产生的影响，用法正确。选项B、C、D中 "impact" 作为动词用法不正确或搭配不当。
7. **C**｜"inevitable" 意为不可避免的，必然的，与 "certain"（确定的、必然的）意思相近。
8. **C**｜"claim responsibility" 意为声称承担责任。
9. **C**｜"otherwise" 意为否则、不然，表示如果不做某事会产生的结果。
10. **C**｜"策略" 在此语境中最佳的翻译是 "strategy"。

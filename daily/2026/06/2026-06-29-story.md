## 中英穿插故事

在浮云市，市民委员会召开紧急会议，讨论一个关于“天空清洁”的荒诞提案。主席指出，他们急需找到确凿的evidence（证据）来证明这个用巨大真空吸尘器吸走低空云层的计划是可行的。这项前所未有的任务需要committee allocate（分配）出一笔considerable（相当大的）资金，用于研发和部署那些飞行器。有人担心，这种人为干预可能会产生严重的consequence（结果），比如导致永恒的晴朗，让人们忘记雨水。然而，另一派则坚称，这项计划拥有巨大potential（潜力），能彻底改变浮云市的旅游业。他们还必须找到方法去maintain（维持）吸尘器的工作效率，避免在高空发生意外。moreover（此外），有居民把最近发生的“微型彩虹雨”attribute（归因于）去年失败的“彩云捕捉”项目。科学家们则警告，天空可能无法迅速adapt（适应）如此剧烈的环境变化。参与云朵吸尘机操作员的招募是完全optional（可选择的），毕竟这存在一定风险。

## 全英文版

In the whimsical city of Nimbusville, the Citizen's Council held an emergency meeting to discuss an absurd "Sky Cleansing" proposal. The chairman emphasized the urgent need to find concrete evidence to prove the feasibility of this audacious plan: vacuuming low-lying clouds with colossal sky-hoovers. This unprecedented task would require the council to allocate a considerable amount of funds for the research, development, and deployment of these peculiar flying machines. Some council members expressed concern, warning that such human intervention could lead to a severe consequence, like perpetual sunshine making people forget the joy of rain. However, another faction insisted the project held immense potential to revolutionize Nimbusville's tourism industry. They also needed to find a way to maintain the hoovers' operational efficiency and prevent high-altitude mishaps. Moreover, residents had begun to attribute recent "micro-rainbow showers" to a failed "cloud-snatching" project from last year. Scientists warned that the sky might not quickly adapt to such drastic environmental changes. Participation in the cloud-hoover operator recruitment was entirely optional, considering the inherent risks involved.

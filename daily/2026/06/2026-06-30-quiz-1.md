# 2026-06-30｜10 词自测（第 1 套）

## 1. She has the _______ to become a great leader, but needs more experience to realize it.

A. A. probable
B. B. certain
C. C. potential
D. D. actual

## 2. Failing to prepare adequately for the exam will have serious _______ for your final grades.

A. A. intentions
B. B. outcomes
C. C. benefits
D. D. causes

## 3. The new policy is highly controversial among the public. _______, it may not be effective in the long run.

A. A. However
B. B. Therefore
C. C. Moreover
D. D. Although

## 4. Which of the following sentences correctly uses the word "maintain"?

A. A. He decided to maintain his old car despite its age, keeping it in good working order.
B. B. She maintained a new hobby last month by trying gardening for the first time.
C. C. The company maintained a new product line by launching it into the market.
D. D. They maintained a difficult relationship with ease, finding it uncomplicated.

## 5. Which word is closest in meaning to "considerable"?

A. A. minor
B. B. trivial
C. C. significant
D. D. negligible

## 6. Choose the best English translation for "把成功归因于努力工作"?

A. A. contribute success to hard work
B. B. attribute success to hard work
C. C. distribute success to hard work
D. D. institute success to hard work

## 7. It took some time for her to _______ to the new working environment after moving to a different city.

A. A. adopt
B. B. adjust
C. C. accept
D. D. adapt

## 8. Which of the following best translates the word "optional"?

A. A. 义务的
B. B. 唯一的
C. C. 可选择的
D. D. 必要的

## 9. There is no concrete _______ to support his claims about the ancient civilization.

A. A. argument
B. B. evidence
C. C. statement
D. D. opinion

## 10. What is the meaning of the word "allocate"?

A. A. 评估
B. B. 分配
C. C. 替代
D. D. 合作

## 答案与解析

1. **C**｜这里需要一个形容词来修饰成为伟大领导者的可能性，'potential'（潜在的）符合语境。
2. **B**｜'consequence' 意为“结果，后果”，此处'outcomes'（结果）是其近义词，符合语境。
3. **C**｜'moreover' 意为“此外，而且”，用于添加更多信息或论点，符合句子的递进关系。
4. **A**｜'maintain' 在此意为“维修”或“维持（良好状态）”，选项A正确表达了维修汽车的含义。
5. **C**｜'considerable' 意为“相当大的，相当多的”，'significant' 也有重要或大量的含义，是其近义词。
6. **B**｜'attribute to' 意为“把……归因于……”，是正确的表达方式。
7. **D**｜'adapt to' 意为“适应……”，是符合句子所需表达的动词词组。
8. **C**｜'optional' 意为“可选择的，非强制的”，C选项是其准确翻译。
9. **B**｜这句话需要一个表示“证据”的词，'evidence'（证据）符合语境。
10. **B**｜Allocate意为“分配，分派”。
